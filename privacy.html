Kyumi Bot Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Kyumi Bot collects, uses, and protects your data:

1. Information We Collect

We collect data such as:

  • Your Discord user ID;
  • Commands you issue to the bot;
  • Interaction logs to enhance bot functionality.

2. Use of Information

Data collected is used to:

  • Provide and improve bot features;
  • Ensure smooth operation and troubleshoot issues;
  • Monitor and prevent misuse of the bot.

3. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. User Rights

You have the right to request access to or deletion of your data. Contact us if you have any questions or concerns about your data.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will inform you of significant changes via the bot or our website.
